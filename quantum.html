My work at Oak Ridge National Labs with Dr. Eugene Dumitrescu and at NC State with Dr. Lex Kemper is focused on efficient gate decompositions for hamiltonian simulation. The first document is our pending publication applying Cartan Decomposition to Hamiltonians spanning a small dimensional Lie Algebraic space. The second document was produced for a course on quantum computing with Dr. Moody Chu, and summarizes methods for universal decomposition of an arbitrary unitary matrix into quantum gates.
